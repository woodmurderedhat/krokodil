What we do

Welcome to krokodil.africa! We are a dedicated team of animal welfare advocates who are passionate about saving crocodiles and alligators that are in need of assistance. Whether they have been injured, displaced from their natural habitat, or confiscated from illegal wildlife trade, our organization is here to help. We provide top-notch medical care and a safe environment for these magnificent creatures to recover, and work tirelessly towards releasing them back into the wild when they are strong and healthy enough to thrive on their own. Our mission goes beyond rescue and rehabilitation, we also strive to educate and raise awareness on crocodile conservation among the American public and the importance of protecting these species that are a vital part of our natural heritage. Browse our website to learn more about our organization and how you can get involved and make a difference.

How you can help us

As a non-profit, we rely on the support of people like you to carry out our important mission of rescuing, rehabilitating, and releasing crocodiles and alligators. There are many ways that the public can help us in our efforts. Here are a few ways you can get involved:

Donate: Financial contributions are crucial for us to continue our work and provide top-notch care for the crocodiles in our care. Your donation will help us to purchase food, medical supplies and equipment, and cover the costs of rehabilitation and release.

Educate: Spread the word about crocodile conservation and the importance of protecting these species. Share our website and social media posts with your friends and family, and encourage them to get involved as well.

Advocate: Reach out to your local elected officials and express your support for crocodile conservation efforts.

Together, we can make a real difference in the lives of crocodiles and alligators and help ensure a future for these magnificent creatures.
