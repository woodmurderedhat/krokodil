Every day, crocodiles face a number of threats to their survival. Habitat destruction, illegal hunting, and pollution all take a toll on these ancient and magnificent animals. Our charity is dedicated to protecting crocodiles and their habitats through rescue, rehabilitation, and conservation efforts. We need your help to continue this critical work.

Your donation will directly support our field teams as they work to rescue and protect crocodiles in danger. Whether it's providing medical care for an injured crocodile, or protecting a nesting site from development, every dollar you give will make a real and meaningful impact. Just $50 can provide food for a rescued crocodile for a month, $100 can go towards purchasing a GPS tracking device for field monitoring and $500 can support the construction of a habitat for a crocodile in need.
